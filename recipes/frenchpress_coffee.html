French press coffee

Comforting tasty coffee

Make the best coffee you've ever tasted with the french press!

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp ground coffe beans
  • 400ml water

Steps

  1. Put 3 tbsp of ground coffee at the bottom of your french press
  2. Put water on the stove until it boils
  3. Gently pour 400ml of water on top of your ground coffee
  4. Mix
  5. Wait for 4 minutes
  6. Slowly but firmly press the mix
  7. Pour hot and enjoy :)
