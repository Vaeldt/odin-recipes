Scrambled eggs

Fluffy scrambled eggs

Make the absolute perfect scrambled eggs with this quick and easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • 3 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs open inside a clean bowl
  2. Beat the eggs until everything is homogeneous
  3. Add some salt and pepper
  4. Oil the pan and put it on the stove
  5. When the oil starts crackling, pour the eggs
  6. Slide the cooked eggs to the middle and keep doing so until everything is cooked
  7. Once everything is cooked but still moist, remove the pan frome the stove
  8. Enjoy your perfectly cooked eggs!
